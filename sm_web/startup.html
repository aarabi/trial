Services
Entrepreneurs and micro small and medium enterprises (MSMEs) count on us to provide advisory and capacity building services for business plan development, team building, fund raising, and coaching and mentoring.


Financial & Business Advisory

  • We guide you to develop business plan, formulate strategy and build teams.
  • We work with the you to raise capital investment – equity, debt, angel, grants.
  • We are empanelled with SIDBI as Accredited Consultants to help you tread through various debt mechanism of SIDBI.



Market and Competitive Strategies

We provide you with company specific operational and strategic advisory services such as

  • Market Entry/Exit Strategies
  • Growth & Shifting Priority Strategy
  • Technology Commercialization
  • Technology Transfer, and
  • Connection between innovation and commercialization partners.



Capacity Building for MSMEs

  • We coach and mentor you and your team to guide you through various stages and phases of your enterprise.
  • We provide training for domain specific and financial skills.
  • Our training and capacity building activities are conducted by practicing professionals who bring you real life experiences to enhance your learning.

Capacity Building for Incubators

  • We work with incubators to mentor and guide the incubated organizations.
  • We guide the incubators to successfully nurture the incubated enterprises.
