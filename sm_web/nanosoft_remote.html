Remote Operations & Management of Micro-Grid

Our patented NanoSoft Remote is a comprehensive solution to manage power generation and consumption in a Micro-grid electrified village
  • Monitor and control renewable hybrid power generation
  • Monitor and control power usage by consumers
  • Manage demand to meet limited supply of power
  • Manage user-controlled demand and power schedule
  • Manage customer relations such as billing, collection, complaints and resolutions
  • Monitor and maintain consumer appliances
  • Train personnel on operations and maintenance

State-of-the-Art Technology

NanoSoft Remote utilizes state-of-the-art telecommunication, information and mobile technologies to deliver its functionalities and open source architecture for ease of development and maintenance.
  • It communicates through a state-of-the-art board, NanoARM, equipped with multiple modes of communication, viz., Ethernet, WiFi, RF, ZigBee, PLC, fiber-optics.
  • It integrates SCADA, GIS, SGIP, WSN protocols for universal compatibility and security, and can interface any energy devices or equipment utilizing SGIP standards.
  • It seamlessly integrates with existing energy metering, power generation or sensor applications following RS232/RS485 protocols, without the need for expensive upgrades.
  • It can be accessed with full functionality of remote operations from any hand held device or standard computer, with net connectivity.
  • It has been developed as a open platform to test new technologies and products to address climate change and energy security.
  • NanoSoft is an independent stand-alone monitoring and management software and can be used as a third party monitoring system for any RE and EE project by project developers, regulators, governments, donor agencies, policy makers.
